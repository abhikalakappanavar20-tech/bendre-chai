About Bendre Chaha – Hubli

Bendre Chaha began its journey in Hubli as a humble tea shop called Galpi, founded by three visionary partners: Sanjay, Maruti, and Ravi. Driven by a passion for authentic tea and quality service, they created a tea experience that Hubli residents could trust and enjoy.

From this modest start, Bendre Chaha became known for its high-quality tea, warm service, and inviting atmosphere. The growing popularity inspired the founders to expand into a franchise model, enabling other entrepreneurs to join a trusted and successful brand.

Today, Bendre Chaha proudly operates 22 branches across Hubli, serving thousands of satisfied customers daily. Built on the values of quality, consistency, and community, the brand supports franchise partners with training, marketing, and operational guidance. Bendre Chaha is more than just tea—it is a Hubli-born legacy of entrepreneurship, passion, and excellence.
